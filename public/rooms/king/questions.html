King Questions

WIP note: reformat to new standard.

All questions you can ask the King in ACT 3 and 4, including variations for quest progress and Acts.

All questions, excluding "What's Wish Craft?", "What did you wish for?" and "Flower for you." are not available after completing Kingquest.

For the finale of Kingquest, see "Let's stop fighting."
